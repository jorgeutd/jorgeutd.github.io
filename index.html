About

I build production AI systems end to end — multi-agent architectures, on-device and cloud LLM/STT inference, MCP tooling, and the evaluation, observability, and release governance that make them safe to run. I’ve built, deployed, monitored, and run evaluations for systems serving 100,000+ users. 12+ years operating at Staff+/Principal level across data science, ML engineering, and GenAI in retail, insurance, and healthcare.

My work spans agents in production (multi-agent swarms serving tens of thousands of daily interactions), on-device inference on Apple Silicon with llama.cpp and whisper.cpp, Model Context Protocol servers over real business systems, fine-tuning and alignment of open models for function calling, and the LLM-as-judge evaluation and checksum-governed release controls that keep it all trustworthy.
